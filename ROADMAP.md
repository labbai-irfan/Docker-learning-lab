# 🗺️ LEARNING ROADMAP

A suggested pacing & milestones for 12–16 weeks of focused study.

---

## Recommended pace

- **Weeks 1–2:** Phase 0 (Foundations) — 40 hrs
- **Weeks 3–4:** Phase 1 (Docker Core) — 30 hrs
- **Weeks 5–7:** Phase 2–3 (CLI, Dockerfile) — 40 hrs
- **Weeks 8–9:** Phase 4–5 (Networking, Storage) — 30 hrs
- **Weeks 10:** Phase 6 (Compose) — 20 hrs
- **Weeks 11:** Phase 7 (Swarm, K8s bridge) — 20 hrs
- **Weeks 12–13:** Phase 8–9 (Security, Observability) — 40 hrs
- **Weeks 14:** Phase 10–11 (CI/CD, Production) — 25 hrs
- **Weeks 15:** Phase 12 (AI Infrastructure) — 25 hrs
- **Week 16:** Phase 13 (Capstone, job prep) — 20 hrs

**Total: ~300 hours of focused study**
