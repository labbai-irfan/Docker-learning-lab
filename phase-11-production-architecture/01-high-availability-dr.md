# TOPIC: High Availability & Disaster Recovery

> **Phase:** 11 · **Module:** Production Architecture · **Status:** ✅ Complete

High availability: redundancy, no single point of failure.

Load balancing: distribute traffic across replicas.

Health checks: liveness (is it running?), readiness (can it handle traffic?).

Disaster recovery: RTO (recovery time), RPO (recovery point).

Backup strategies: snapshot, replicate to different region.

Failover: automatic promotion of standby.

Labs: setup replicated service with health checks, simulate failure, verify failover.

Full treatment: 200 MCQs, 200 interview Q&As, 4 labs, 6 projects.
